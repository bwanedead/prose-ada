# Big Picture

High-level thinking about the story, its themes, structure, and overarching vision.

## What Goes Here

- Story arc and structure
- Major themes and motifs
- Character development across the entire story
- World-building foundations
- Plot threads and how they weave together
- The "why" behind the story

## Relationship to Other Directories

- **`vision/`** - More focused brainstorming and vision documents
- **`big-picture/`** - Broader, strategic thinking about the story as a whole
- **`chapters/`** - Specific chapter work
- **`brainstorm-dump/`** - Raw, unorganized ideas

Think of this as the strategic overview, while `vision/` might be more tactical planning.

# References

Research materials, historical texts, academic sources, and reference documents for Prosse Ada.

## What Goes Here

- Historical texts and primary sources
- Academic papers and research articles
- Books and scholarly works
- Mythology references (especially Norse mythology/Prose Edda)
- Cultural and historical context materials
- Any source material that informs the story

## Organization

Organize however makes sense:
- By topic or theme
- By source type (books, articles, etc.)
- Chronologically
- Or keep it simple and flat

## Relationship to Other Directories

- **`references/`** - Source materials and research documents
- **`chapters/*/references/`** - Chapter-specific references
- **`research/`** - If you create one, this would be more general research notes
- **`inspiration/`** - Creative inspiration (references here are more academic/source material)

# Brainstorm Dump

A free-form space for dumping all your brainstorm ideas, thoughts, and inspirations without worrying about organization.

## Usage

Just dump anything here:
- Random ideas
- Stream-of-consciousness thoughts
- Inspiration snippets
- Quick notes
- Whatever comes to mind

Don't worry about structure or organization - this is your creative dumping ground. You can organize things later into `vision/`, `notes/`, or specific chapters as they develop.

## File Naming

Name files however makes sense to you:
- `random-thoughts.md`
- `ideas-2024-01-15.txt`
- `inspiration-dump.md`
- Or just `brainstorm-1.md`, `brainstorm-2.md`, etc.

Whatever works for your flow!
